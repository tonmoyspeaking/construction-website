About Us

Two Decades of Infrastructure Excellence

Founded in 2004, KD Constructions is a leading multi-disciplinary engineering company specializing in government infrastructure across Maharashtra. We deliver complex civil, mechanical, fabrication and electrical projects for India's most critical railway and urban development initiatives.

Fully self-owned equipment fleet
In-house civil, mechanical, fabrication & electrical teams
Zero-defect, on-time project delivery
Presence in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Bhusawal & Dhule

Our Vision

To be a trusted engineering partner delivering sustainable, safe and high-quality infrastructure that strengthens India's transportation and urban development.

Our Mission

Deliver zero-defect, on-time projects while ensuring safety and compliance. Build long-term relationships with government bodies. Maintain transparency, integrity and accountability.
